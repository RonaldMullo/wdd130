Our History

The history of whitewater rafting dates back to the early 19th century, with the first recorded attempt to navigate the Snake River in 1811. However, the popularization of rafting as a sport and recreational activity occurred in the 1950s and 1960s, with the emergence of rafting companies and its inclusion in the 1972 Olympic Games.

Our company was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate river guides who wanted to share their love for whitewater with the world. Today, we continue that legacy with the same passion and commitment to safety and adventure.
