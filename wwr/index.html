Our History

The history of whitewater rafting dates back to the early 19th century, with the first recorded attempt to navigate the Snake River in 1811. However, the popularization of rafting as a sport and recreational activity occurred in the 1950s and 1960s.

Our company was founded in 1985 by river enthusiast Mark Waters, who turned his passion into a thriving adventure business that has introduced over 50,000 people to the joy of whitewater rafting.
